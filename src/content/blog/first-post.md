Hello there! Firstly, a warm welcome to you. If you're reading this, I can only assume that you either know me very well and appreciate whatever crap that I've been working on or you are just a randomer with a high level of curiousity and some spare time. Apart from that, I don't think anyone else would actually even consider reading this. If you fall outside of both these categories, then I hope you will introduce yourself to me because I would genuinely be intrigued.

Anyway, let's focus on today's blog. Being the first ever post, it will just be a short introduction covering what this is and a prelude to what you can expect moving forward. Recently, I've realized that I have too many thoughts and perspectives that mostly gets dumped to nowhere so I wanted a way to record them somewhere. And so blogs came to my mind which made me contemplate on beginning my blogging arc.

I got curious and started exploring the blogging world. The insights I gained were fascinating and I realized blogging is actually very simple and straighforward. Told myself: "bet, I can do this then". The only concern was "where do I post my write-ups?" So I digged deeper and what started as a research to find the best blog posting platform quickly turned into asking myself "why do I need other platform when I can just create my own?"; hence, I leveraged my personal website and extended it with a blog section.

And tada here we are with the first ever post written exclusively on my website. Probably a big milestone because I've been procrastinating this for quite some time, but let's not celebrate it too early. Consistency is key, so we will see how it goes!

Right... so what can you expect here? To be honest, I don't even know myself. All I can foresee is a mix of everything. My thoughts, opinions, perspectives, anything that interests or irritates me, story of my life and experiences, educational content, predictions, and literally anything else covering various topics. The posting frequency will be random too. Will any of these be useful to you a.k.a. the readers? Probably not, but it's a matter of subjectivity and I'll leave that to you guys.

One thing I can assure is I will not use AI here. I have signed an agreement to myself to **NOT** use our favourite AI chatbots like Chatgpt or whatever to influence my write-ups. Every write-up for the most part will be pure, unpolished and completely natural. So if you're here for perfect english, exquisite usage of grammar, seamless flow and continuity between sentences, or even eloquently expressed thoughts and ideas, then you're at the wrong place. Expect the complete opposite with a touch of randomness and some chaos.

_Disclaimer: AI was used for other contents on these website. Only the blog section will be written by me._

With that being said, I guess I'll just wrap this up for now. Feels like I could go on and on and on, but short and concise write-ups are always appreciated because it consumes less time for both the writer and the reader. Okay adios guys as I abruptly end this blog.

Have a good day!
